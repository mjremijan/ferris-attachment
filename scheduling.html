Scheduling to Run

Linux

Use CRON to schedule the running of the application. 

# Run Attachment every weekday morning
30 6 * * mon,tue,wed,thu,fri /home/username/ferris-attachment-1.0.0.3-linux-x86-64-jre/bin/start-attachment.sh -unsent /tmp/unsent -sent /tmp/sent -to someone@somewhere.com -subject "An attachment" > /dev/null 2>&1

Happy attaching!
